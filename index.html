Mission

Our mission is to improve the quality of life of our clients and their families by helping them find acceptance,
guidance, and hope through delivering evidence-based psychological interventions tailored to meet their needs.
We also aim to offer the latest evidence based training to mental health professionals in the region.

Vision

We strive to advance the profession of Psychology in Oman, make a difference in people's mental health and wellbeing,
and have a society which actively seeks mental health services and supports people on their journey to positive mental health.

About Our Services

Eunoia offers confidential, evidence-based psychological services to the community.
At Eunoia we employ numerous methods to seek out early indicators of mental health conditions
to allow for intervention before a problem progresses. If a condition already exists,
we are able to apply the expertise of our specialized mental health professionals through your journey towards recovery.
Our professionals will work closely together to formulate the best treatment strategy for you.
We provide diagnosis and treatment across the entire spectrum of mental health but focus on clinical psychology,
which is concerned with the assessment, diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of mental disorders including:

  • Generalized Anxiety Disorder
  • Panic Disorder
  • Phobias
  • Acute Stress Disorder
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder
  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorder
  • Depressive Disorders
  • Bipolar Disorder

In addition, we offer practical support for the following life challenges:

  • Stress
  • Sleep Difficulties
  • Relationship Difficulties
  • Grief & Loss

All cases are reviewed to determine appropriateness for the clinic.
Below is a list of what the clinic does NOT provide:

  • Emergency or walk-in appointments
  • Child custody evaluations or psychological evaluations in cases involving litigation
  • Services for chronic substance use and dependence
  • Services for medically involved eating disorders
  • Court-ordered treatments
  • Services to individuals with serious mental illnesses who are noncompliant with medication

The clinic is not a crisis center and therefore is not equipped to meet the needs of individuals who require 24-hour support.
If we are not able to meet your needs you will be referred to a center that is better able to assist you.

Professional Training, Consultancy and Clinical Supervision

Eunoia offers a range of consultation, training and supervision services for mental health professionals including
in-house training, individual supervision and Internships and Clinical Placements.
In addition, we have developed our own training programs (Therapy Tips & Beginners Tips)
targeting therapists in the field and novel mental health practitioners.
Our psychologists are registered trainers in mental health.
